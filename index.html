#about-me

Hello, I'm Eslam!

I am a self-taught front-end developer based in Qalyubia, Egypt. My expertise lies in building responsive websites from scratch and transforming them into modern, user-friendly experiences.

For over 5 years, I have pursued my creative passion—merging design and code to deliver impactful digital solutions. I believe every pixel and every line of code can tell a story when crafted with precision.

I am currently a Team Leader at Taktek, guiding a dynamic team to create innovative news platforms, podcast portals, and cutting-edge web applications. My approach revolves around continuous learning, whether it's adopting new frameworks or exploring the latest trends in UI/UX.

I'm always eager to collaborate with like-minded professionals and clients to build digital products that resonate with users and elevate brands.
